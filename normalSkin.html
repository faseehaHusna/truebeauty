    How to choose a good cleanser ?

  • Select a non-comedogenic cleanser to prevent clogging pores.
  • Choose a gel-based or cream cleansers
  • Choose a cleanser with a pH close . to that of the skin (around 4.7 to 5.75).

    How to choose a good moisturizer ?

  • Look for Moisturizer that contains lightweight formulas.
  • Choose a moisturizer that contains hydrating ingredients such as hyaluronic acid, glycerin, or aloe vera.
  • Choose fragrance-free moisturizers to minimize the risk of any adverse reactions.

    How to choose a good sunscreen ?

  • Select a sunscreen with a Sun Protection Factor (SPF) of at least 30.
  • Look for sunscreens with skin-friendly ingredients.
  • Choose a texture that feels comfortable on your skin and suits your lifestyle.
